Prevalence of Mental Health Disorders in Tech

575 people out of the 1433 who answered this question say they currently having a mental disorder. This is 40% of the survey population. All of these people are employed, most at technology companies. Of the 287 people who answered the next question, 204 say that their mental disorder affects their productivity. Obviously, this is something that companies should address, for both alturistic reasons and practical ones.
